2024 Fall and 2025 Spring Comps!

Thank you to everyone who participated in the INTEGIRLS DFW 2024 Fall and Spring Competition! We truly appreciate all the hard work of the INTEGIRLS DFW team and our generous sponsors, and because of them, we were able to host 300+ girls and non-binary people from all over the world. Additionally thanks to Bay Area for our great partnership for our Spring 2025 Comp!

It was remarkable to see the culmination of months of planning and preparation result in such a successful competition. We hope our competition was able to foster a feeling of community or belonging in every attendee, whether a first-time or seasoned competitor. We are incredibly proud of all participants for choosing to challenge themselves.

Special thank you to AoPS, Wolfram, Jane Street, Bank of America, and Girls Who Code and many other amazing companies for sponsoring and donating prizes.

We hope to see everyone at our next competition in Fall!
